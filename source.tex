	$$\ket{\phi\psi} = \alpha_\phi \alpha_\psi \ket{00} + \alpha_\phi \beta_\psi \ket{01} + \beta_\phi \alpha_\psi \ket{10} + \beta_\phi \beta_\psi \ket{11}$$
	$$\ket{\zeta\eta} = \alpha_{00} \ket{00} + \alpha_{01} \ket{01} + \alpha_{10} \ket{10} + \alpha_{11} \ket{11}, \; \sum_{i=00}^{11}{|\alpha_i|^2} = 1$$
	$$\ket{\nu_0 \nu_1 ... \nu_n} = \sum_{i={00..0}}^{11..1}{\alpha_i \ket{i}}, \; \sum_{i={00..0}}^{11..1}{|\alpha_i|^2} = 1$$
